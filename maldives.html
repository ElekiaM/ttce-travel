Maldive Island Destination Description.

The Maldive Islands are a series of coral atolls built up from the crowns of a submerged ancient volcanic mountain range.

All the islands are low-lying, none rising to more than 6 feet (1.8 metres) above sea level. Barrier reefs protect the islands from the destructive effects of monsoons.
