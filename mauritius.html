Mauritius Destination Overview

Known for its beaches, reefs and lagoons, Mauritius has been inviting thousands of tourists from all over the world. A place where palm trees bent towards the white shimmering sand amidst the Indian Ocean. A widely recognised place as a paradise on Earth offers a perfect blend of relaxation and luxury, with adventure and excitement. Be it the chic beach parties or the adrenaline rushing activities, this place lets you have a perfect tropical escape. TTCE Tours & Travel Center will take you to this beautiful tropical paradise and will make sure that you enjoy every second of your vacation.

Mauritius Honeymoon Tour Package Itinerary.
DAY 1

Start the beautiful adventure that you will cherish for eternity.

Arrive at the Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport and meet our representative who will drive you to your hotel.

Witness the beauty of this tropical island on your way to the hotel.

The volcanic island of lagoons and palm-fringed beaches with coral reefs surrounding most of the coastline.

Proceed with the check-in formalities at the hotel and enjoy your day at leisure.

You can indulge in shopping from the local markets and explore nearby attractions or simply relax at the hotel.

Overnight stay at the hotel.
DAY 2 Dolphin Encounter Tour | Cruise along the Wester Coast.
DAY 3 The Ultimate Discovery Tour of Chamarel | Relish the tropical nature at its best.
DAY 4 Day at leisure | Stroll around the nearby places to mesmerise the beauty of the place or indulge in a thrilling adventure of Seakart.
DAY 5 Departure | Time to head back home with a plethora of memories.
Inclusions:

Accommodation: Comfortable stay for 4 nights in a luxurious hotel.

Meals: Breakfast included as mentioned in Itinerary.

Guide: Professional English speaking guide to help you explore the cities.

Transfers: All transfers by car as per the itinerary.

Visa Assistance: It will be provided (Charges are not included).

Experiences Covered:

Embrace an amazing Dolphin Encounter Tour.

Sail along the west coast of Mauritius and spot dolphins.

Witness the beautiful and colourful marine life while indulging in snorkelling.

Delicious barbecue lunch & unlimited local drinks.

Witness the beautiful Chamarel Waterfall and seven coloured earth.

Witness the sight of a dormant volcano, Trou Aux Cerfs.

Opt to experience some thrill during Seakart adventure.

Things not to miss:

Stroll around bare feet on the white sand beaches of North Island.

Hike Black River Gorges National Park for sweeping coastal views.

Hike to the top of Le Morne, a UNESCO-listed mountain for heavenly views of coral gardens.

Try Casela Natural Park Segway tour.

Relish Mauritian Rum.
