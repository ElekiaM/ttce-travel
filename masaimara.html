Itinerary.

Day 1 Nairobi – Masai Mara

Departure from Nairobi in the morning for a scenic drive down into the Rift Valley, to Masai Mara, Kenya’s premier game reserve. Stop briefly en-route at the Great Rift Valley viewpoint for stunning views of what is the world’s largest valley. Arrive at Masai Mara in time for lunch. The afternoon game drive allows spotting of the unrivaled wildlife in the park. Return to your camp for overnight.

Day 2 Masai Mara National Reserve.
5Masai Mara National Reserve

Full day spent at the Masai Mara game reserve. We drive across the plains in search of the big cats (black manned lions, Cheetahs and leopards with picnic lunch served at the Mara River site. Vast herds of wildebeest and zebra wander across the Mara grasslands in huge numbers during this period of the annual migrations take place, when millions of wildebeest, zebra, and gazelle traverse the Mara plains landscape to feed on the rich new grass from the Serengeti. You also have a chance to see other plain like Buffallos,giraffes,Hippos,Rhinos,Impalasa and for those love birders we have Vultures,secretary bird superb sterling,kingfishers and many more. Late we may have an opportunity for masai village visit to make our tour special Dinner and overnight at budget camp/ luxury camp

Day 3 Masai Mara- Nairobi

After an early breakfast we have an early morning game drive in maasai mara as this will be the last day you will be in the famous reserve. After the short morning game drive, you drive to Nairobi you will make a stopover at maimahiu for lunch then later drive to Nairobi and arrive late afternoon and check in at your hotel and that’s mark the end of our safari
